Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

AI Web Reader ("we", "our", or "the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

AI Web Reader collects and processes the following data:

  • Page Content: When you click "Analyze", the text content of the current webpage is extracted locally in your browser.
  • Chat Messages: Questions you type into the chat interface are processed to generate AI responses.
  • Page URL: The URL of the analyzed page is used as an identifier to associate indexed content and chat history.

We do not collect any personal information such as your name, email address, browsing history, cookies, or IP address.

2. How We Process Your Data

  • Local Embedding: Page content is split into chunks and converted into numerical embeddings (TF-IDF vectors) entirely within your browser and our backend server. No third-party embedding service is used.
  • AI Responses: Only the small, relevant text chunks needed to answer your question are sent to the AI language model API (Cerebras). The full page content is never sent to any third party.
  • In-Memory Storage: All indexed content and chat history are stored in server memory and are automatically deleted when the server restarts or when you close the associated browser tab.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your data to any third parties. The only external service that receives data is the AI model provider (Cerebras), which receives:

  • Small relevant text chunks from the analyzed page (not the full content)
  • Your chat question

This data is sent solely to generate a response and is subject to Cerebras' Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage & Retention

  • All indexed page data and chat history are stored in-memory only — nothing is written to a database or persistent storage.
  • Data is automatically cleared when the server restarts.
  • Chat history for a page is removed approximately 60 seconds after you close all tabs viewing that page.

5. Permissions

The extension requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • activeTab: To access the content of the currently active tab when you choose to analyze it.
  • scripting: To inject the chat widget interface into web pages.

These permissions are the minimum required for the extension to function. We do not request access to your browsing history, bookmarks, or any other browser data.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data:

  • Communication between the extension and backend uses standard HTTP protocols.
  • No data is persisted to disk — all processing is ephemeral and in-memory.
  • The extension does not inject any tracking scripts or analytics.

7. Children's Privacy

AI Web Reader is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the Extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
